Why Northwind

Three things we won't compromise on.

Direct-trade beans

We buy every lot directly from the farms we visit each year. No brokers, no opacity on the price the farmer received.

🔥

Roasted to order

Beans are roasted the week you order them. The bag is dated, not generic. Stale coffee is the easiest problem to avoid.

📦

Shipped fast

Orders placed by Monday ship Wednesday. Free shipping on bags of 12 oz or larger anywhere in the U.S.
